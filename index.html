Montblanc Meisterstück

Montblanc Meisterstück Classique Rose-Gold-coated collection is a design that writes history. Introduced in 1924, a word meaning “Masterpiece,” the Meisterstück has become the symbol for high quality luxury writing.

Montblanc Meisterstück

The cap and barrel are crafted in black precious resin featuring the iconic white Montblanc emblem inlaid in the cap top. The fountain pen is adorned with a hand-crafted Au585/14K solid gold nib, bi-color rhodium and rose gold-coated.
